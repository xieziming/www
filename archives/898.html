咳咳，要有情调！

07 March 2009 Categoried in 点滴 13 Preview : 127 , Read : 874 Index : 2334

傍晚吃饭时，商务的严研MM建议去她喜欢的那家特有情调的餐厅，我们这几个男生也不好说啥，去！

到了才发现，原来又是那家老挝餐馆…..

严MM发话了，“这次我们要吃的有情调点！”，呵呵，俗人当久了，情调不是说有就有的。

要了3个老挝煎饼，1盘Fire Chicken，1份Pizza，1樽鸡尾酒，最要还要了一瓶Dark Beer。

“咳咳，有MM在的时候要有情调”，严MM言传身教开来 …….

不过，那家餐馆还真是能够营造气氛，布置的很好，人也不多。楼下桌球台边的MM甚是漂亮，啧啧！

心情很好，回来顺便就把PM发过来的邮件改了下，感谢小钱钱与sunny。

Dear all，
I’m writing this letter for the LTC SDH Project Phase14.

By Feb.10th, 2009, we have finished hardware installation of 73 sites. And the left 6 sites are not equipped because of inappropriate environment for installation,such as no power supply.

According to our progress, we can complete the project by Mar.9th, 2009 according to the contract. In our plan, we have arranged our experts and the subcontracts to start PAC at anytime.

However, the main problem we are facing now is that the fiber is not ready until now, without which the whole project will be dadly delayed.

So I am wondering if it is possible for LTC to give us PAC firstly. Your understanding upon this matter would be highly appreciated. We ensure to complete the task in timewith the first-class quality.

Thank you for your consideration! If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact with me.

Yours sincerely,

David Zhang
