In Louangnamtha

08 April 2009 Categoried in 旅行 6 Preview : 312 , Read : 1809 Index : 2241

Ur leader MinG MinG has disappeared for quite a long time, but guys, don’t miss me~ i am fine. Now, i am in Louangnamtha, bounded on the north by China, on the west by Thailand and Burma and on the east by Vietnam. Detailed information is shown in the following map.

That’s to say, now, i am quite close to China mainland. Hehehh, i got it when i found that there are so many Chinese restaurants and shops. Quite more than other provinces. This suspect has been verified by the map.

It’s quite like a single-handed journey, i am out by myself, expert the driver, no local staff accompanied. But i am not alone, once had arrived at the capital of the province, the LTC(Lao Telecommunications) local engineers would keep me accompany, though their english are quite bad. Local engineer of LTC in Oudomxai accompanied me can only speak, “sir”, “ok”, “optical power”, etc. quite simple phrase. OMG.

?I has arrived at Louangnamtha 5 days ago, but no progress till now for the fiber is not ready. Many funny things happened during these days. The LTC engineers from Vientiane were left behind in Oudomxai for cut over and the driver cannot speak english, so who care for my diet?! The first two days i went to the indian restaurant. Why? Cos, the waiter can speak english, and it has menu. Hehehh, fire rice with seasonal chiken(25,000K), then fire rice with eggs(20,000K), certainly, Pepsi is need. I went to a Lao food restaurant at the third day, but found that there wasn’t any menu, and it was difficult to make the waiter understand what i want to eat. Finally, i was given a bowl of Lao noodle(8,000K). Fortunately, i found a Chinese restaurant yesterday, the boss can speak Chinese. The Chinese fire rice with meat(10,000K), Soup with meat and bean curd(20,000K) and the greengrocery(10,000K).

?Nothing to do during these 5 days, so, how to spend time idly? Surfing the net? No, it’s quite expensive to get on the internet in this province, 12,000K/H. Staying at the guest house and wathing tv? No, it’s quite boring. Goofing around? Sounds a good idea, but you know, i am not familiar with this city, so it was 3 days latter when i decided to goof around. Firstly, to rent a bicycle, quite cheap here, 8,000 ~ 15,000K/Day, if you want to rent a motorbike, ok, 30,000K ~ 50,000K is enough. What you need to do is to fill in a form, and pay the money(just the rent money, you don’t need to pay any guarantee deposit ). That means you pay 8,000 ~ 15,000K, you can take away a quite nice bike without any guarantee deposit, which is impossible in China. Credit!! Secondly, camer a is also needed.

During the trip, i bought a waterman at the roadside, quite cheap, only 2,000K(1.6 RMB).
